Direction

Working business shape

Lead with trade

Bars, lounges, hotel bars, and event bartenders should come first. They offer clearer reorder behavior and a more practical entry point than broad consumer launch messaging.

Stay narrow early

Launch with preserved citrus wheels first. Avoid trying to debut too many enhancers, ice systems, or retail formats before production quality is stable.

Keep the website useful

Customer site for ordering and overview. Project notes for internal use. Pitch pack for clear online presentation. Each surface should stay simple and distinct.

Live channels
