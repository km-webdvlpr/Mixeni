Direction

Recommended business shape

Lead with trade

Bars, lounges, hotel bars, and event bartenders should come first. They have clearer reorder behavior and give MIXENI a stronger commercial wedge than broad consumer launch messaging.

Stay narrow early

Launch with preserved citrus wheels first. Avoid trying to debut too many enhancers, ice systems, or retail formats before production quality is stable.

Keep the website useful

Public site for orders and credibility. Founder hub for planning and execution. Pitch pack for readable storytelling. Each part has a clear job.

Operations

What must exist before scale

Machinery priorities

  • Dedicated dehydrator
  • Mandoline or commercial slicer
  • Heat sealer
  • Digital scale
  • Storage and moisture control

Systems priorities

  • Batch log for each run
  • Simple SKU and pack-size structure
  • Lead-time and delivery promise
  • Trade sampling workflow
  • Basic label and traceability approach
