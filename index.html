Low-friction model

Keep the first version simple

No full ecommerce yet

You do not need payment gateways and cart logic before you have repeatable production. Manual channel-based ordering is enough to start and much easier to control.

Use direct channels

WhatsApp and email are enough for early velocity. They also help you learn order patterns, delivery expectations, and product questions quickly.

Deliver like a premium service

The brand has to feel composed: clear lead times, neat packaging, and a reliable response rhythm. That matters more than a complex storefront right now.

The customers you should pursue first

Best early-fit customers

  • Premium bars and cocktail lounges
  • Hotel bars and restaurants
  • Event bartenders and curated private functions
  • Corporate gifting buyers during seasonal peaks

Why this matters

These customers reorder and care about consistency. They are much more useful than chasing broad consumer traffic too early because they give you operating proof, not just attention.
