Business plan

Condensed written version

Concept

MIXENI is a South African premium cocktail finishing brand centered on preserved citrus wheels and related garnish elements. The value lies in consistency, speed, lower spoilage, and luxury-coded presentation.

Commercial wedge

Premium bars, hotels, restaurants, lounges, and event bartenders should come first because they produce repeat consumption and clearer operational proof.

Initial products

Lemon, lime, orange, and grapefruit wheels in trade pouches first. Home packs and signature gift sets follow later.

Operating model

Source fruit, slice consistently, dehydrate to a defined standard, pack with traceability, and fulfill orders through direct channels until broader infrastructure is justified.

Funding logic

What the raise should unlock

Production

Dedicated dehydration, slicing, sealing, and workspace readiness.

Packaging

Premium pouches, jars, boxes, and launch inventory without overcommitting on volume.

Go-to-market

Trade sampling, content, delivery flow, and the first recurring accounts.
